CARBON CALC

about

Hi! I'm a sixteen-year-old high school student with a passion for coding and a love for our planet. I created CarbonCalc to combine these interests and make it easier for people to understand and reduce their carbon footprint. I started carbonCalc because I wanted to make it easier for people to see the difference they can make in their daily lives. By understanding your carbon footprint, you can identify areas where small changes can lead to big results. Whether it's cutting down on energy use, reducing waste, or making more sustainable choices, carbonCalc is here to help you every step of the way. I'm excited to share this journey with you and to use technology to empower positive change. Together, we can work towards a more sustainable future, one week at a time!
